# Life Planning Pillar Additions — Integration Index

## Purpose

This package consolidates the new pillar and addendum drafts discussed for the Life Plan Tracker application.

The existing application is framed as a lifetime development tracking system for children ages 0–35, using pillars, milestones, notes, age bands, family economy concepts, and structured planning documents. These additions are designed to fit that architecture: each document can be dropped into `docs/`, used to seed new milestones, and later expanded into dedicated dashboards, records, and diagrams.

## Recommended New Pillars

### 1. Dimensional Thinking, Perception & Life Navigation

Internal name:

```text
dimensional_navigation
```

Purpose:

Develop the faculty of perception across time, space, systems, consequence, and hidden effects. This pillar distinguishes dimensions from navigational forces and treats religion as humanity's ancient study of consequence, perception, action, and reverberating effects.

### 2. Civic, Economic & Institutional Navigation

Internal name:

```text
civic_economic_navigation
```

Purpose:

Teach how society actually functions across voting, money, markets, ownership, local institutions, civic duty, and distributed influence.

### 3. Scientific Method & Reality Testing

Internal name:

```text
scientific_reality_testing
```

Purpose:

Teach the scientific method as disciplined reality testing, not merely academic science. Includes introspective testing, course correction, claim confidence, and institutional humility.

### 4. Inheritance, Burden & Stewardship

Internal name:

```text
inheritance_burden_stewardship
```

Purpose:

Teach the child to receive inheritance with gratitude, audit inherited burden honestly, repair what can be repaired, refuse what should not continue, and pass forward something cleaner.

### 5. Catholic Sacramental Formation & Domestic Church

Internal name:

```text
catholic_formation
```

Purpose:

Track Catholic milestones, sacramental records, domestic church practices, authority literacy, and safe-environment formation.

### 6. Secular Sacred Formation & Civilizational Literacy

Internal name:

```text
secular_sacred_formation
```

Purpose:

Provide a bridge for formation when belief is absent, rejected, uncertain, wounded, or later recovered. The child can be atheist and still come home.

## Recommended Addenda

### Lost Knowledge, Corrupted Knowledge, and Institutional Blindness

Attach to:

```text
scientific_reality_testing
```

Purpose:

Teach how knowledge can be discovered, tested, forgotten, corrupted, resisted, rediscovered, or abandoned to charlatans when serious institutions become arrogant.

### Recidivism, Forgiveness, and Forgetting

Attach to:

```text
secular_sacred_formation
catholic_formation
inheritance_burden_stewardship
```

Purpose:

Teach that forgiveness, forgetting, and recidivism are related but distinct. Forgiveness does not mean amnesia. Remembering harm does not mean refusing mercy. Repeated harm requires pattern recognition and protection.

### Life Cards & Life Maps

Place in:

```text
docs/19_life_cards_life_maps.md
```

Purpose:

Provide dashboard-level formation maps that frame the whole life arc and the widening circles of responsibility. The Life Chart Card shows where a life sits in time; the Expanding Breadth of Belonging chart shows where responsibilities sit in relation to others. The Breadth of Belonging ring work belongs under the Formation Study bounty program.

### Jury Duty & Controversial Case Review

Attach to:

```text
civic_economic_navigation
formation_study
summer_capstone
```

Purpose:

Create repeatable civic-literacy bounties where the child reviews controversial court cases with juror discipline: charges, burden of proof, admitted evidence, excluded evidence, public narrative, courtroom record, verdict, moral questions, institutional questions, and family presentation. This should be seeded as no-decay repeatable bounties under `jury_case_review`, with the full 4–8 week version also surfaced through Summer Capstone Projects.

## Guardrail

These documents should not be converted into ideological indoctrination. They are formation maps. The child should be taught gratitude without blindness, skepticism without cynicism, forgiveness without unsafe amnesia, and faith without coercion.
