# Heritage & Identity Board

## "Who Do You Think You Are?"

Not genealogy trivia. A lifelong investigation into:

- Where did we come from?
- What shaped us?
- What burdens do we carry?
- What gifts did we inherit?
- What traditions are worth preserving?
- Which ones should end with us?

---

## Domain: Origins

**Questions:**
- Who were our ancestors?
- Where did they live?
- What languages did they speak?
- What religions did they practice?
- What wars, migrations, and hardships did they experience?

**Categories:**
- Family Tree
- Geography
- Migration
- Historical Events
- Cultural Practices

---

## Domain: Language

**Categories:**

## Heritage Roles (Progression)

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Explorer | Collects stories |
| Archivist | Preserves records |
| Researcher | Verifies facts |
| Historian | Connects events |
| Steward | Passes knowledge forward |

---

## Annual "Who Do You Think You Are?" Packet

Every year the child completes:

- **One Ancestor** — Research one person
- **One Place** — Research one location
- **One Story** — Record one family story
- **One Artifact** — Digitize or preserve something
- **One Tradition** — Understand where it came from
- **One Lesson** — What should be passed to the next generation?

## The Final Answer

The answer isn't ultimately a DNA percentage or a family tree.

The final answer becomes:

> **"What forces, people, beliefs, sacrifices, languages, and stories made me who I am, and what part of that inheritance am I responsible for carrying forward?"**
